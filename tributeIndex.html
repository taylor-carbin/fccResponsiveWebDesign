Stevie Nicks' life & legacy

  • 1948 - Born on May 26 in Pheonix, Arizona

  • 1964 - Nicks received a guitar for her 16th birthday which she used to write her first song.

  • 1967 - Lindsey Buckingham asks Stevie to join his band, Fritz, marking the beginning of their musical patnership.

  • 1973 - Nicks and Buckingham release their duo album, Buckingham Nicks, with little success.

  • 1975 - Nicks and Buckingham join Fleetwood Mac. The band released its self-titled albumn which was globally well-received. The album included Nicks' songs, Rhiannon and Landslide.
